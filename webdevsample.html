E-Commerce HIFI Sample

During our second year of studies, we collaboratively designed and developed a high-fidelity website solely using HTML and CSS for our Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) subject. This project served as a practical application of HCI principles, allowing us to apply user-centric design and usability concepts. Working as a team of three, we meticulously crafted the website's user interface and ensured it adhered to best practices in web development. Through this project, we gained hands-on experience in creating aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly web interfaces while honing our skills in HTML and CSS. In the end, our high-fidelity website project demonstrated our ability to translate HCI theory into a functional and visually appealing digital product
